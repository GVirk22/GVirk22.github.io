I have lived in Burnaby for 17 years. While in Burnaby, I went to Elementary and Highschool where I have made friends that I am still close with. Some of my favourite memories were hanging out with friends and family. During the summer, we often went to Cultus Lake and PNE. I would often go with my family and extended family. My early life can be summed up by spending time with my brothers, cousins, and friends. It was fun for the most part, and I made some great memories.
