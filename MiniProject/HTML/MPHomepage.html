About Me

My name is Gurdit and I was born on October 22nd, 2004. I am 3rd year student currently enrolled in SFU's Computer Science program. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I have worked with many programming languages. These languages include Javascript, Python, C++, and Assembly. I have worked on a few projects associated with these languages mostly individually, but sometimes collaboratively.

My time as an undergraduate student so far has taught me a lot about myself. I came into this program with a very minimal knowledge of coding. However, throughout my undergraduate studies, I have come to enjoy it. Problem solving is something I have always enjoyed. It is always satisfying to take on a difficult problem and find a solution. It also pushes me to learn more as each programming language has different requirements and present unique solutions. This is what I like most about Computer Science and why I enjoy coding.
